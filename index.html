Cooking Game

System Flow of Pamana

Pamana is a 2D single-player fast-paced cooking simulator game developed in Unity for PC. Players are immersed in the beautiful sceneries of the Philippines while delighting on the food and their culture through cooking and serving customers. The game mixes time-pressured gameplay with a rich story line that will enhance the immersive feel of the game. Its gameplay mainly revolves around exploring, cooking, earning for upgrades, and completing goals to advance to the next level.
